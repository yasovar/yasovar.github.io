My Work Experience

Indian Institute of Remote Sensing , Dehradun

Software Developer Intern

Jun 2021 - Aug 2021

Initial work was based on Global Navigation Satellite System Reflectrometry (GNSS-R). Signals from a Satellite which bounce of the surface where the antenna is placed and gets received by the antenna.

Work also involved the prediction of characteristics of storms such as trajectories, internsity and size.

Surface Features

Characteristics such as Soil Moisture, Snow Height from surface, height of tidal waves if situated close to a water body.

Extraction Method

Used Fourier analysis on Signal to Noise Ratio Magnitude to find signals with frequency that correspond to reveal the required data from the antenna surface.

Storm Prediction

Trained data on BiLSTM followed by LSTM which is later followed by Time Distributed Dense Networks to predict trajectories, intensity and size of storms.
